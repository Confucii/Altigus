Overview

COVID-19, War in Ukraine, Global crisis… Our world can sometimes seem so cruel and uncomfortable to live in… Once I asked myself a question: how could we make the world a better place? Could we really change it? And I believe the answer is absolutely positive: yes, we can! But to do it we should start from changing ourselves. We all should become stronger, faster, cleverer — better at whatever we want to improve. Self-improvement is a key. And that is why I decided to start working on the Altigus project. Together we will build the best social network the world ever saw. Based on cooperation and willingness to both self- and world-improvement, it will change the way we think, the way we do things, and even the way we live… Or at least it just will be fun. Hope to see you in my project, wether as the platform user, software co-creator or project sponsor. Whoever you are and will become, I wish you good and productive life!

-Yegor Varchenko,

leader of Altigus project
